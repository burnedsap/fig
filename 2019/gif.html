Idea Jam
Behind every mature idea are numerous smaller ones that got stuck and never came into being. Those sparks that don't ever light up, those original thoughts that fit nowhere and those story plots that don't have enough muscle to grow a beginning, middle or end. And what happens when we force something to happen? It doesn't. Till all the pieces aren't in place, the idea will not bear fruit. My GIF is a representation of this very part of the creative process. When we REALLY want something to work but have trouble concluding it, we often force our hand and mess things up. The resulting mess may be useless now, but who knows, perhaps parts of it will help in realising an idea jam later some day! Additionally, the fact that frustrating paper jams happen so often — obstructing the flow of work — also adds a layer of very real annoyance to the GIF!
